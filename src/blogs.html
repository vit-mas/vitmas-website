Every time your phone predicts the next word you're about to type, or a streaming service guesses what you'll watch next, there's a good chance a Markov chain is quietly working behind the scenes. These models capture how systems move from one state to another based purely on the present, not the past, making them deceptively simple yet remarkably powerful. From weather forecasting to detecting anomalies in user behavior, Markov chains turn uncertainty into something we can actually reason about
